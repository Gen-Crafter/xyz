HIPAA Privacy Rule: PHI Definition and Minimum Necessary Standard

Protected Health Information (PHI):
- Individually identifiable health information in any form or medium.
- Includes direct and indirect identifiers tied to health context.

Minimum Necessary:
- Access and disclosure should be limited to minimum data needed.
- Workforce access must align with role and purpose.

Operational mapping:
- Analytics and vendor workflows must strip or mask identifiers when full PHI is not required.

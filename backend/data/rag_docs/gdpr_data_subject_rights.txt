GDPR Data Subject Rights (Curated Operational Notes)

Article 17 Right to Erasure:
- Data subjects may request deletion when legal conditions apply.
- Erasure can be limited by legal obligations or public-interest exceptions.
- Controllers must document reason when refusing deletion.

Article 18 Restriction of Processing:
- Processing may be restricted while disputes are assessed.
- Restricted records must be clearly marked and controlled.

Article 20 Data Portability:
- Data subjects can request portable data in structured formats.

Article 22 Automated Decision-Making:
- Significant automated decisions require safeguards and rights to contest.
- Additional transparency and human review controls are recommended.

Operational mapping:
- For conflict cases, map deletion request to retention obligations and record decision rationale.

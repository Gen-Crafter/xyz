Cross-Regulation Conflict Map: GDPR vs PCI-DSS

Conflict:
- GDPR Articles 44-49 constrain transfer of EU personal data.
- PCI DSS Requirement 3 requires strict protection of account data when retained.

Bridge approach:
- Tokenize or format-preserving-encrypt PAN before transfer.
- Use SCC or adequacy mechanisms when EU-origin data crosses borders.
- Restrict transfer to minimum fields required for card-processing objective.

Citations:
- GDPR Art.44, GDPR Art.46, PCI DSS Req 3.3, Req 3.4.

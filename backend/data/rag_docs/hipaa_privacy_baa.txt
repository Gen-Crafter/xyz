HIPAA Privacy and Security Rule: Business Associate Agreement Requirements

Sections 164.308(b)(1) and 164.502(e):
- A Business Associate Agreement (BAA) is required before vendor PHI access.
- Agreement must define safeguards, permitted uses, and breach duties.

Operational mapping:
- Vendor or analytics access without BAA should be blocked or quarantined.
- Restore access only after BAA and technical safeguards are validated.

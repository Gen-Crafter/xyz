PCI Compliance Exposure and Cost Impact (Curated Notes)

Observed impact patterns:
- Ongoing non-compliance can trigger recurring financial penalties and card-brand escalations.
- Remediation costs include incident response, forensics, re-architecture, and audit cycles.
- Training environments unintentionally brought into PCI scope create hidden control debt.

Operational lesson:
- Detect and block raw PAN usage early in data-science workflows.

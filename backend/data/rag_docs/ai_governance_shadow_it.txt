AI Governance: Shadow IT Controls for External LLM Usage

Risk pattern:
- Business teams send employee or customer data to unapproved external AI endpoints.

Required controls:
- Route all AI calls through approved enterprise gateway.
- Enforce DPA validation for external processors.
- Require lawful basis and transfer mechanism for EU personal data.
- Block unknown endpoints and create near-miss audit trace.

Citations:
- GDPR Art.6, Art.28, Art.44, Art.33.

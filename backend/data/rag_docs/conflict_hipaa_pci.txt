Cross-Regulation Conflict Map: HIPAA vs PCI-DSS

Conflict:
- HIPAA and PCI both require strong logging and control evidence, but retention and log format expectations can differ.

Resolution pattern:
- Maintain dual-archive logging strategy with mapping index.
- Ensure both healthcare and card-data events are retained per policy.
- Apply unified incident response process with domain-specific evidence packs.

Citations:
- HIPAA 164.312(b), PCI DSS Req 10.2, PCI DSS Req 10.7.

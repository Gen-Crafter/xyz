HIPAA Privacy Rule requires covered entities to protect individually identifiable health information and limit use and disclosure to the minimum necessary purpose.
HIPAA administrative requirements include workforce training, safeguards, and designated privacy responsibilities.
HIPAA documentation retention requires relevant policies and procedures to be retained for six years from creation or effective date.
HIPAA Security Rule requires administrative, physical, and technical safeguards for confidentiality, integrity, and availability of ePHI.
NIST SP 800-66r2 guidance maps HIPAA safeguards to risk management practices and emphasizes continuous assessment and auditability.

Cross-Regulation Conflict Map: GDPR vs HIPAA

Conflict:
- GDPR Article 17 supports erasure requests.
- HIPAA operational and documentation obligations can require retention.

Resolution strategy 1: Selective de-identification.
- Remove direct identifiers while retaining minimum lawful record set.

Resolution strategy 2: Pseudonymized vault retention.
- Store restricted reference data in controlled vault with legal access controls.

Resolution strategy 3: Policy-based deletion workflow.
- Evaluate legal exceptions, record decision rationale, and execute approved deletion scope.

Citations:
- GDPR Art.17, GDPR Art.6, HIPAA 164.502, HIPAA 164.308.

GDPR Records, Processor Agreements, and Incident Duties

Article 28 Processor Controls:
- Controller-processor relationship requires binding data processing terms.
- Processor must follow documented instructions and safeguards.

Article 30 Records of Processing Activities (ROPA):
- Maintain records of processing purpose, categories, and transfers.

Article 33 Breach Notification:
- Notify supervisory authority within required timelines for personal data breach.

Article 35 DPIA:
- Conduct DPIA for high-risk processing and significant scope changes.

Operational mapping:
- External AI tool use requires DPA review.
- Model drift and new personal-data fields should trigger DPIA checks.

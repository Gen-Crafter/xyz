GDPR Cross-Border Data Transfers (Articles 44-49)

Article 44:
- Any transfer to third countries requires GDPR-level protection.

Article 46:
- Appropriate safeguards may include Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

Articles 45-49:
- Adequacy decisions can permit transfer to approved jurisdictions.
- Derogations are limited and must be narrowly interpreted.

Operational mapping:
- Export from EU workloads requires adequacy or SCC evidence before release.
- Missing transfer mechanism should trigger block and remediation workflow.
